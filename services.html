Treatment of teeth and gums


Healthy teeth and gums ensure well-being and overall health of the whole body. With regular preventive dental examinations and treatment of diseases in the initial stages, you will be able to rid yourself of many problems and risks, ranging from discomfort and sudden pain, ending with tooth loss and improper functioning of the digestive organs. At the IDent clinic, you will receive high-quality dental treatment with modern equipment. In the treatment and re-treatment of dental canals, the clinic's specialists use the latest Leica M320 dental microscope.

Hygiene and prevention


Dentistry is one of the most developed medical fields, which has a large number of techniques for preserving teeth under the most difficult conditions and stages of the disease. However, if it is impossible to restore the affected tooth, dental specialists of IDent carry out its careful removal. All surgical procedures are performed by experienced dentists using high-quality instruments and effective painkillers.

Surgical dentistry


Effective hygiene and prevention of the oral cavity includes not only systematic compliance with recommendations for home care of teeth and gums, but also regular special hygiene procedures. Constant care and attention to the condition of the teeth keeps them healthy for many years, and also makes a visit to the dentist pleasant and economical.

Implantation


An implant is an element that serves as a reliable basis for an artificial tooth, which is installed in place of the lost one. In the dental clinic of IDent, you can use the implant placement service, as well as a set of related procedures necessary for successful implantation and subsequent prosthetics

Prosthetics


Prosthetics is one of the most important and popular areas of dentistry, with the help of which a specialist can restore the integrity and functioning of the patient's dentition. Experienced orthopedists of the clinic use modern techniques and equipment, including a dental microscope to prepare teeth for prosthetics, as well as effective anesthesia and only high-quality materials for removable and conditionally removable prosthetics.

Diagnostics


The success of any treatment, first of all, lies in the correct diagnosis. IDent dentistry has the most accurate diagnostic technologies that allow to quickly and accurately determine the presence of dental and gum diseases in a patient, even at the initial stages, as well as to choose the most effective method of their treatment.

Aesthetic dentistry


Aesthetic dentistry, unlike traditional dentistry, is designed to create a flawless and beautiful smile that will allow its owner to forget about embarrassment and stiffness in emotions and communication. At the IDent clinic, you can get a range of services for the complete transformation of a smile — changing the color and shape of teeth, replacing old, volumetric composite restorations, discoloration, as well as reducing the distance between teeth.

Correction of bite

Malocclusion and uneven dentition is a common problem that is subject to effective treatment in both adults and children. An experienced orthodontist will take you to the dental clinic of IDent, who will choose the most optimal way to correct the bite and irregularities of the teeth.

Ediatric dentistry

Treatment of baby teeth is a process that requires a special approach to a young patient. Our specialists will conduct medical manipulations in a relaxed playful way, after which your child will understand that treating teeth is not painful and not scary at all. You will also receive valuable recommendations on the specifics of baby food and prevention of oral diseases.
