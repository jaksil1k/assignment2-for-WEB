Our clinic


HIGHLY QUALIFIED DOCTORS

INNOVATIVE EQUIPMENT

Our doctors are our pride! The average practical experience of the clinic's doctors exceeds 20 years. Every year our specialists undergo foreign internships in countries such as Japan, Switzerland, Germany, Ukraine and Russia.

The use of the latest technologies in dental treatment, which has practically no analogues in the Republic of Kazakhstan (CAD/CAM systems, Straumann implants, AIRFLOW Prophylaxis Master, ZOOM whitening).

CURATOR OF TREATMENT FOR EACH PATIENT

CERTIFIED, LICENSED MATERIALS

The curator of treatment will help you plan the entire treatment process in the clinic: the frequency of visits and the budget for services based on your needs, remember about planned procedures in time and receive high-quality individual service

Consumables and equipment from leading manufacturers in the USA, Japan, Germany, Switzerland, have passed clinical trials and have quality certificates and licenses.

FOCUS ON QUALITY

PATIENT SAFETY

We are able to solve any task of transforming the smiles of adults and children from the treatment of caries to the most complex surgical operations

The clinic staff annually undergoes an emergency care course. Also, the clinic has a sterilization department; quartz treatment is carried out in the premises, and the air in the lobby is constantly disinfected with a bactericidal recirculator.
